Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsman in Cricket history...He currently holds the record for the most runs scored in both Odis and Test cricket..He was part of the indian team when India lifted the Odi world cup in 2011.

Sourav Ganguly

Sourav Ganguly was an indian cricketer who played for India in the late 90s and the early 2000s..He was widely regarded as the captain who raised the indian team to its current fame..He was the captain of India when india reached the final but unfortunately lost

Ms Dhoni

Mahendra singh Dhoni was an indian cricketer who is widely regarded as the best captain in the history of indian cricket, Under his captaincy, Team India lifted all 3 icc trophies T20 in 2007, Odi in 2011 and champions Trophy in 2013..he is also known for his lightning quick stumpings in international cricket

Virat Kohli

Virat kohli is an indian cricketer who is primarily a batsman..He is widely regarded as the greatest batsman in modern day cricket. He was part of the indian team when india lifted odi worldcup in 2011,Champions Trophy in 2013 and T20 worldcup in 2024 and also the champions trophy in 2025..He is currently retired in T20s and tests
